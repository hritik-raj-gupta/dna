‚öôÔ∏è How DNA Fingerprinting Works

1. Sample Collection

Blood, saliva, hair, skin cells, or any biological material

2. DNA Extraction

DNA is isolated from cell nucleus using chemical processes

3. DNA Amplification

PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) creates millions of copies

4. Restriction Cutting

Restriction enzymes cut DNA at specific sequences

5. Gel Electrophoresis

DNA fragments separated by size using electric field

6. Pattern Analysis

Unique banding pattern compared with known samples

üß™ Gel Electrophoresis Process

How DNA Fragments are Separated

DNA fragments move through gel matrix when electric current is applied. Smaller fragments travel faster and farther than larger ones.
